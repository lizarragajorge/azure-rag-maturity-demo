# Pad-Mount Distribution Transformer — PM Schedule

Applies to three-phase pad-mount transformers rated 75 kVA through 2500 kVA
serving commercial and light-industrial customers on the 12.47 kV and 25 kV
distribution feeders.

## Annual visual inspection

Performed without de-energization. The crew records:

1. Cabinet door alignment, padlock condition, and tamper-seal integrity.
2. Oil-level sight glass reading and ambient temperature.
3. Visible oil leaks at the bushings, drain valve, or pressure-relief device.
4. Vegetation clearance — minimum 10 ft clear in front of doors, 3 ft on sides
   and rear.
5. Grounding conductor continuity from the X0 bushing to the ground grid (visual
   plus 25-ohm-or-less ground resistance test every five years).

## Triennial infrared scan

A thermographic survey on all secondary connections and the low-voltage bushing
stems. Any delta-T greater than 10°C above ambient on a connector requires a
work order within 30 days; greater than 30°C requires same-shift action.

## Five-year dissolved gas analysis (DGA)

Pull a 100 mL oil sample through the drain valve into a glass syringe and ship
to the lab within 72 hours. The interpretation follows **IEEE C57.104-2019**:

| Gas        | Normal (ppm) | Caution     | Action          |
|------------|--------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Hydrogen   | < 100        | 100 – 700   | > 700           |
| Methane    | < 120        | 120 – 400   | > 400           |
| Acetylene  | < 1          | 1 – 9       | > 9 (arc fault) |
| Ethylene   | < 50         | 50 – 100    | > 100           |
| CO         | < 350        | 350 – 570   | > 570           |

Any acetylene above 1 ppm is treated as evidence of an internal arc and the
unit is scheduled for replacement.

## On-load tap changer (LTC) — applicable only to 1500 kVA and larger

Operate the LTC through its full range under no-load conditions during the
annual inspection. Counter readings exceeding 5,000 operations since the last
contact inspection trigger an internal contact wear assessment.
